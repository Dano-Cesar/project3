Bienvenido to Puebla

Welcome to my hometown Puebla. The city of Puebla was established in 1531 and was known as Puebla de los Angeles; "Puebla of the Angles." This city is located in east–centtral Mexico and is 83 miles southeast of Mexico City making it less than a 2 hour drive from there. From this city you can seethe Popocatepetl Volcano, its a sight to see

Being the foruth largest city in Mexico, Puebla is a beautiful place to visit thanks to its buildings structures. The houses, buidlings, and walls are built with tiles keeping the cities orgins strong and preserving its religious structures! Making the cities Cathedral and ruins a must see tourist attraction for all to visit.

City Highlights

  • In 1987 the UNESCO declared The Historic Centre of Puebla as the Cultural Heritage of Humanity due its architecture
  • Cinco de Mayo originate in Puebla from the "Battle of Puebla" in May 5, 1862
