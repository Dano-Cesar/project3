Bienvenido to Puebla Mexico

Welcome to Puebla Mexico. The city of Puebla was establised in 1531 and is known as "Puebla de los Angles"; Puebla of the Angles. Located 83 miles southeast of Mexico City, visting Puebla is less than a 2 hour drive from Mexico city. As if you didn't need more reasons to visit this city, Puebla has an active volcano called Popocatepetl, its a sight to see.

Being the fourth larget city in Mexico, Puebla is a beautiful place to vist in east–central Mexico. The homes, buildings, walls, and streets have a beatiful architectural style by their use of tiles! To this day as the city gets morden they still use tiles in hopes to preserving its orgins strongs. The the cities Cathedreal and ruins a must see tourist attraction for all to see
